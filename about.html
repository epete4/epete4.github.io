About Us

We are a group of motorcycle enthusiasts who love to ride together and share our passion for bikes. Our club was founded in 2005 and we've been going strong ever since.

Our members come from all walks of life and ride different types of bikes, but we all share a common bond through our love of motorcycles. We believe in promoting safe and responsible riding, and always obey traffic laws and regulations.

Our club hosts regular group rides, charity events, and social gatherings. We also participate in community events and parades, and work to give back to our local community.

Our Values

  • Respect for others and their property
  • Honor and loyalty to our club and members
  • Responsibility and safety on the road
  • Community involvement and giving back
